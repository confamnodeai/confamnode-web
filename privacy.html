01 Who we are

ConfamNode is operated by JoTeq the First, a technology company based in Abuja, Nigeria. We provide an AI inference gateway that allows Nigerian developers and businesses to access AI models, billed in Naira.

Data Controller: JoTeq the First

Location: Abuja, Nigeria

Contact: hello@confamnode.com

This policy is written in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and the guidance of the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

02 What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

Account information

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a hashed value — never in plain text)

Billing information

  • Wallet balance (Naira)
  • Transaction history — top-ups and deductions
  • Payment references from Paystack (we do not store your card details — Paystack handles this)

Usage information

  • API requests made — model used, timestamp, token counts
  • Cost per request in Naira
  • Your ConfamNode API key

What we do NOT store

  • The content of your prompts and AI responses is not permanently stored beyond what is necessary for billing and abuse prevention
  • Your card or bank account details — Paystack manages all payment data

03 Why we collect it

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contract — to provide the service you signed up for: generating API keys, billing your wallet, tracking usage
  • Legal obligation — to comply with Nigerian financial and tax regulations
  • Legitimate interest — to detect fraud, prevent abuse, and keep the service secure

04 How we use your data

  • To create and manage your ConfamNode account
  • To generate and manage your API keys
  • To process wallet top-ups via Paystack
  • To calculate and deduct usage costs in Naira
  • To send important service updates — billing alerts, security notices
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. ConfamNode products are ad-free.

05 Where your data is stored

  • Account, billing, and usage data is stored on servers located in Nigeria, operated by JoTeq the First
  • Requests made to local models (such as confam-nano) are processed entirely on Nigerian hardware and never transmitted outside Nigeria
  • Requests made to cloud models are routed through third-party AI infrastructure which may process data outside Nigeria. We select providers with strong data protection practices, but data sent to these models is subject to the destination provider's own data handling policies

If you require data to remain entirely within Nigeria, use our local models — they run on Nigerian hardware with full data residency.

06 Third parties

We share limited data with the following processors strictly to operate the service:

Third Party Purpose Data Shared
Paystack Payment processing Email, transaction amount
AI model providers Processing your AI requests (cloud models only) Prompt content sent for cloud models only
Cloudflare Website hosting, email routing Email address, website traffic

We do not share data with any party for marketing or advertising purposes.

07 Data retention

  • Account data — retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account closure request
  • Transaction records — retained for 5 years to comply with Nigerian financial record-keeping requirements
  • Usage logs — retained for 12 months for billing disputes and abuse investigation, then deleted

08 Your rights

As a data subject under the NDPA, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object — object to certain types of processing
  • Data portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact hello@confamnode.com. We will respond within the timeframes required by the NDPA.

09 Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed — never stored in plain text
  • API keys are cryptographically generated and can be revoked at any time
  • Access to production systems is restricted
  • Local models run on isolated, access-controlled hardware

No system is 100% secure. If a data breach occurs that affects your personal data, we will notify you and the NDPC as required by law.

10 Children

ConfamNode is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us at hello@confamnode.com and we will delete it promptly.

11 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on our website. Continued use of the service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
